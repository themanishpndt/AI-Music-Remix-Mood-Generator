## üéµ Popular Remix Styles

### 1. Nightcore Remix
**What is it?** High-pitched, fast-paced version of a song

**Settings:**
- Tempo: `1.4x - 1.6x`
- Pitch Shift: `+3 to +5 semitones`
- Mood: `energetic`
- Genre: `electronic`

**Perfect for:**
- Anime music videos
- High-energy playlists
- Gaming content
- Upbeat remixes

---

### 2. Lo-Fi / Slowed + Reverb
**What is it?** Relaxed, atmospheric version

**Settings:**
- Tempo: `0.7x - 0.85x`
- Pitch Shift: `0 to -2 semitones`
- Mood: `calm`
- Genre: `ambient`
- Add Harmony: `third`

**Perfect for:**
- Study music
- Chill playlists
- Background ambiance
- Relaxation

---

### 3. Dark/Ominous Version
**What is it?** Darker, more intense version

**Settings:**
- AI Mood Transform: `any ‚Üí dark`
- Pitch Shift: `-3 to -5 semitones`
- Tempo: `0.9x`
- Genre: `rock` (for distortion)

**Perfect for:**
- Dramatic videos
- Horror content
- Intense scenes
- Moody playlists

---

### 4. Sped Up / TikTok Style
**What is it?** Fast, catchy version

**Settings:**
- Tempo: `1.2x - 1.3x`
- Pitch Shift: `+1 to +2 semitones`
- Mood: `uplifting`
- Genre: `electronic`

**Perfect for:**
- Social media content
- Short videos
- Viral trends
- Dance remixes

---

### 5. Deep Voice / Bass Boosted
**What is it?** Lower, deeper version

**Settings:**
- Pitch Shift: `-5 to -8 semitones`
- Mood: `dark`
- Genre: `rock`
- Tempo: `0.95x`

**Perfect for:**
- Voice transformation
- Bass-heavy remixes
- Dramatic effect
- Character voices

## üéì Educational Projects

### Project 1: Mood Exploration
**Goal:** Understand how mood affects music

**Steps:**
1. Upload one song
2. Create 6 versions (one per mood)
3. Compare differences
4. Learn mood characteristics

**Learning outcomes:**
- Mood = tempo + energy + brightness
- How filters change perception
- Emotional impact of music

---

### Project 2: Pitch & Harmony Study

2. Create versions with different pitch shifts
3. Add different harmony types
4. Compare intervals

**Learning outcomes:**
- Semitones and intervals
- Harmony relationships
- Chord construction basics
